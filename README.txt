== Description ==

= Introduction =

Ever wanted to sell to a global audience but accept payments locally? Pockyt is an alternative payment platform that unleashes true cross-border growth potential for businesses of all sizes. Equip your online store with modern payment technology that enables you to support all major global wallets with one simple integration; including, Alipay, WeChat Pay, UnionPay, Kakao Pay, Cash App, Paypal, and Venmo. Click [here](https://docs.yuansfer.com/guide/payment-types) to learn more about the wallets, currencies, and transaction types available to you.

= Benefits =

•	Access to over 2 billion brand new customers by accepting global wallets
•	Eliminate chargebacks and reduce fraud with QR based wallets transactions.
•	Reduce Transaction Fees (vs. Traditional card payments)
•	Future proof, support any number of wallets today or tomorrow with no additional development.
•	Improved engagement with Chinese shoppers
•	Most transparent pricing at 2.3% per transaction, with No surcharges, No hidden fees, No monthly fees, and No commitment. (Custom rates available for merchants with large payment volume)

= Technical Features =

•	Full & partial refunds.
•	Pre-authorization or Pre-auth available for hotel, travel, and car rental merchants.
•	Increased security measures to protect both you and your customers
•	Technologies to help reduce your cart abandonment rate to increase sales.
•	Currently only available in the US.

= Process Flows =

•	The customer places an order on your website and chooses to pay with Pockyt.
•	The customer is redirected to the wallet network’s website/App to log in and is prompted to confirm payment within the e-wallet.
•	The payment status is communicated to you in real-time.
•	We remit the collected payments to you in 2 business days.
Ready to get started? Download this plugin and complete this quick Merchant Agreement form online found [here](https://onlinecontract.yuansfer.com/).

Our mission is the connect the world through borderless commerce. And we take pride in growing business from local to global, while bridging the gap between brands in the U.S. and consumers located globally with proven payment solutions.

== Installation ==

Please note, this plugin requires WooCommerce 2.6 and above.

= Automatic installation =

Automatic installation is the easiest option as WordPress handles the file transfers itself and you don’t need to leave your web browser. To
do an automatic install of, log in to your WordPress dashboard, navigate to the Plugins menu and click Add New.

In the search field type “Yuansfer Payment for WooCommerce” and click Search Plugins. Once you’ve found our plugin you can view details about it such as the point release, rating and description. Most importantly of course, you can install it by simply clicking "Install Now".

= Manual installation =

The manual installation method involves downloading our plugin and uploading it to your web server via your favorite FTP application. The WordPress codex contains [instructions on how to do this here](http://codex.wordpress.org/Managing_Plugins#Manual_Plugin_Installation).

= Updating =

Automatic updates should work like a charm; as always though, ensure you backup your site just in case.
